Science Olympiad at MIT is an undergraduate student group that first started out as volunteers to local tournaments in Spring 2022. We were founded by Ryan Anselm, Eric Mao, and Aunika Zheng (2024) for our first tournament of the 2023 season. We passionate about Science Olympiad and want to share our love for the competition with the community. We are entirely run by students and are dedicated to providing a fun and educational experience for all competitors. While our biggest milestone each year is our invitational, we also push to provide resources and volunteer in the surrounding community. If you are interesting in joining contact us and check out our current team. In the end, we want to have fun!
